<h1>【Media Coverage】Featured in GOOD JOB JOURNAL</h1>

<p>
NanoFrontier Inc. (Headquarters: Sendai, Miyagi, Japan; Representative Director: Seiya Inoue) is pleased to announce that a feature article about the company was published in "GOOD JOB JOURNAL" on April 3, 2026.
</p>

<p>
The article, titled "The Future of Materials Development Pioneered by AI: The Research Revolution Envisioned by a Tohoku University Startup," features an interview with Seiya Inoue, Representative Director and CEO of NanoFrontier Inc., a startup founded out of Tohoku University. The article covers the company's multi-sector business expansion built upon nanoparticle manufacturing technology developed at Tohoku University, its efforts toward the automation of research and development using AI and robotics, its achievement in winning the NEDO prize-based program "GENIAC-PRIZE," and its three-axis growth strategy targeting ¥100 billion in revenue within ten years — encompassing business domain expansion, value chain expansion, and overseas expansion.
</p>

<p>
Leveraging its proprietary nanoparticle technology, NanoFrontier Inc. is working to address societal challenges in the environmental, energy, and information infrastructure sectors through the development of detection materials for PFAS environmental contamination, next-generation energy storage materials, and highly thermally conductive nanofluids.
</p>

<p>
We will continue to contribute to the realization of a sustainable society through the advancement of our research and development capabilities and the acceleration of social implementation.
</p>
