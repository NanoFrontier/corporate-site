<h1>【Media Coverage】Featured in Nikkei Cross Tech (xTECH)</h1>

<p>
NanoFrontier Inc. (Headquarters: Sendai, Miyagi, Japan; Representative Director & CEO: Seiya Inoue) is pleased to announce that a feature article about the company was published in Nikkei Cross Tech (xTECH) on April 27, 2026.
</p>

<p>
The article, titled "NanoFrontier Gives Its In-House AI Scientist 'Mechanical Hands,' Tackling Nanoparticles Through Automated Experimentation," covers NanoFrontier's efforts to build a nanoparticle research and development process from the ground up using a proprietary AI scientist (AI agent) designed around generative AI, as well as the company's initiatives to automate R&D workflows by introducing robotics into experimental processes. The article also explores how CEO Seiya Inoue and co-founder Katsuya Saito entered the field of nanoparticle research as software engineers, the company's systematized three-stage research workflow spanning literature search, simulation, and experimentation, and its vision for the full automation of research and development in the future.
</p>

<p>
NanoFrontier Inc. leverages its proprietary nanoparticle technology to develop detection materials for PFAS environmental contamination, next-generation energy storage materials, and high thermal conductivity nanofluids, working to address societal challenges in the fields of environment, energy, and information infrastructure.
</p>

<p>
We will continue to advance our research and development capabilities and accelerate the real-world implementation of our technologies, contributing to the realization of a sustainable society.
</p>
