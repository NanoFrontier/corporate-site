## NanoFrontier raises approximately 150 million yen through third-party allotment

NanoFrontier Co., Ltd. (Headquarters: Sendai City, Miyagi Prefecture; Representative Director: Seiya Inoue) completed a funding round of approximately 150 million yen through third-party allotment in October 2025.

The investors in this round include three venture capital firms and one corporate investor:

- Genesia Ventures, Inc.
- Animal Spirits LLC
- Mobile Internet Capital, Inc.
- Nitto Boseki Co., Ltd.

Additionally, the company has secured over 100 million yen in grants, including support from the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry and the Small and Medium Enterprise Agency's R&D Support Program for Growth-Oriented SMEs (Go-Tech Program).

![Funding image](https://prcdn.freetls.fastly.net/release_image/163636/16/163636-16-63138378d523ee71aa81014f02be90fb-3900x2048.png?width=1950&height=1350&quality=85%2C65&format=jpeg&auto=webp&fit=bounds&bg-color=fff)

## Use of funds

The raised funds will be primarily used for the following purposes:

1. Establishing a research and development base within Tohoku University
2. Accelerating PoC for organic nano-dye reagents capable of immediate on-site colorimetric detection of specific harmful substances such as certain PFAS
3. Developing continuous mass production equipment for nanoparticles
4. Setting up an automated research and development pipeline
5. Strengthening recruitment (in synthetic chemistry, analysis and quality assurance, data engineering, and operations)

In particular, the company will focus on real-time detection of specific harmful substances such as certain PFAS, advancing the development of technology that instantly visualizes interactions with targets as optical responses using organic dye nanoparticles.
