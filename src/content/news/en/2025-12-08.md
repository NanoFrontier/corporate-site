## NanoFrontier Secures Grant for Innovative PFAS Detection Technology

NanoFrontier Co., Ltd. (Headquarters: Aoba-ku, Sendai, Miyagi Prefecture; Representative Director: Seiya Inoue) has been selected for the "28th 77 New Business Grant" organized by the Public Interest Incorporated Foundation 77 Business Support Foundation. The company's proprietary nanoparticle generation technology, which enables low-cost and real-time detection of specific PFAS (such as PFOA and PFOS), was highly evaluated for its novelty, originality, and business potential.

With this grant, NanoFrontier will establish a continuous mass production system for more controlled nanoparticles, contributing to the early detection of PFAS contamination and the construction of a safe water environment.

### About the 77 New Business Grant

The "77 New Business Grant" is a support program implemented by the Public Interest Incorporated Foundation 77 Business Support Foundation to promote industrial development and new business creation in Miyagi Prefecture and the Tohoku region. It aims to contribute to sustainable regional economic development by supporting companies and organizations with innovative business plans rooted in the region, backing their initial efforts towards business realization.

## Reasons for Selection

The main reasons why NanoFrontier's technology was highly evaluated are as follows:

- Addressing global regulatory tightening on PFAS health impacts
- Innovativeness of the "organic nano-dye for specific PFAS detection" developed by Tohoku University
- Realization of mass production and stable supply through the company's advanced nanomaterial continuous purification technology
- Potential to contribute to early detection of environmental pollution and construction of safe water infrastructure
