<h1>【Media Coverage】Featured in Polishing Hub</h1>

<p>
NanoFrontier Inc. (Headquarters: Sendai, Miyagi, Japan; Representative Director & CEO: Seiya Inoue) is pleased to announce that a feature article about the company was published in Polishing Hub on April 14, 2026.
</p>

<p>
The article, titled "Redefining Materials Development with Nanoparticles and AI: The Full Story of Tohoku University Startup NanoFrontier," covers the versatility of the company's proprietary nanoparticle manufacturing technology and its technology-licensing-focused business model, the commercialization of a simplified PFAS detection kit, the automation and acceleration of research and development processes through AI and robotics, the company's award at NEDO's prize-based program "GENIAC-PRIZE," the background and career of Representative Director & CEO Seiya Inoue and the founding story of the company, cumulative fundraising of over 250 million yen and a three-axis growth strategy (expansion of business domains, expansion of the value chain, and overseas development), and the company's novel approach to regional revitalization as it pursues global markets from the Tohoku region.
</p>

<p>
NanoFrontier Inc. leverages its proprietary nanoparticle technology to develop detection materials for PFAS environmental contamination, next-generation energy storage materials, and highly thermally conductive nanofluids, working to address societal challenges in the fields of environment, energy, and information infrastructure.
</p>

<p>
We will continue to advance our research and development capabilities and accelerate the social implementation of our technologies, contributing to the realization of a sustainable society.
</p>
