<h1>【Media Coverage】Featured in Mobile Internet Capital (note)</h1>

<p>
NanoFrontier Inc. (Headquarters: Sendai, Miyagi, Japan; Representative Director & CEO: Seiya Inoue) is pleased to announce that an introductory article about the company was published on April 10, 2026, in "Mobile Internet Capital (note)."
</p>

<p>
The article, titled "MIC NIGHT Report," covers the pitch presentation delivered by NanoFrontier Inc. Representative Director & CEO Seiya Inoue at "MIC NIGHT 2026," an exclusive invitation-only event hosted by Mobile Internet Capital Co., Ltd. (MIC) on March 16, 2026. The article introduces NanoFrontier's efforts to build a mass-production architecture grounded in nanoparticle technology originating from Tohoku University, the acceleration and automation of research and development processes leveraging AI, the development of simplified PFAS testing solutions, a range of application areas including rare-metal-free rechargeable batteries and cooling fluids for data centers, and the significance of NanoFrontier as a startup rooted in the Tohoku region.
</p>

<p>
NanoFrontier Inc. leverages its proprietary nanoparticle technology to develop detection materials for PFAS environmental contamination, next-generation battery materials, and highly thermally conductive nanofluids, working to address societal challenges in the fields of environment, energy, and information infrastructure.
</p>

<p>
We will continue to contribute to the realization of a sustainable society through the advancement of our research and development and the acceleration of social implementation.
</p>
